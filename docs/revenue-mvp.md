# Revenue MVP

## Free diagnostic

- Gemini sample diagnostic
- max 3 buyer-intent queries
- limited report with source metadata and honest provider coverage
- full-audit CTA

## Full audit

- up to 20 buyer-intent queries
- Gemini + ChatGPT + Claude when configured
- competitor share of voice
- query-level breakdown
- listing fixes
- PDF/shareable report later

## Positioning

SEO tells you where you rank on Google.

AnswerRank tells you where you rank inside AI buying recommendations.

## Manual monetization path

The product does not need billing on day one to become revenue-ready.

Early path:

- run free sample diagnostic
- capture lead from report CTA
- deliver manual full audit
- use full-audit findings to justify recurring tracking later
